My Cats

Zeke

Zeke was my first cat. He was a
rescue from a shelter in my hometown
of Findlay, Ohio.
He was found on the side of the road.

Jax

Jax was my second cat. I got him a few
months after adopting Zeke so he would
have a playmate. Jax was unfortunately
hit by a car when he was a kitten and
brought in with a severely broken leg.
It required screws right below his knee
joint to fuse the leg back together.
